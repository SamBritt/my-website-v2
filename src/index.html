Artwork

Since I was a child, drawing and painting has allowed me to take the abstract ideas that swim in my head and turn them into something beautiful. Over the years, I've developed the knack for drawing people, mainly faces. From sketching the outline of a head all the way to perfecting the reflected light underneath a nose, I've found that the process of structuring and shaping a head piece by piece is very similar to the approach of designing a web page. Just as all web pages start with HTML, every person I create starts with a simple ellipse.
